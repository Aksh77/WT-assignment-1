Architecture

The School started in the year 2011 with an intake of 40 students, under the directions of the Directorate of Technical Education, Government of Kerala, with the benevolence of the Hon'ble Chief Minister of Kerala Shri.Ommen Chandy. Now it has the all five batches of Students also with the number of students growing upto 200.

Civil Engineering

The Department of Civil Engineering has been in existence since the inception of RIT. The department offers B. Tech course in the field of Civil Engineering with an annual intake of 60 students. The activities of the department include, Civil Engineering consultancy, testing and certification. The staff members are involved as resource persons for various development projects in the state. Most of our students are either placed in reputed companies or selected for higher studies through GATE. The department has several university ranks to its credit. Students regularly organises national level technical symposium- Vaastheya, which provides a platform for interaction among students.

Computer Science and Engineering

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering has recorded a consistent growth. The Department was established in 1999, and now has become a full-fledged department with advanced laboratory facilities to train the students to meet the current needs of the fast changing industrial scenario.The Computer Science &Engineering Department provides excellent environment for training in computer hardware and software. With ever increasing demand for software and hardware professionals in IT field , the course provides ample opportunities for employment in India and abroad.The department offers four year B. Tech course and two year M.Tech course in Computer Science & Engineering.

Computer Application (MCA)

The Institute offers three years Masters Degree in Computer Applications from 2001 onwards. The course is affiliated to Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam with an intake of 60 students. The MCA Department has well-equipped networked computer laboratories with latest computer systems and software packages, which enable the students to meet the challenges of the IT industry.

Electrical and Electronics Engineering

The Electrical and Electronics Engineering Department provides excellent environment for training in electrical and electronics engineering. With ever increasing demand for energy and newer areas of energy production, the course provides ample opportunities for employment in India and abroad.The department offers four year B. Tech course in Electrical and Electronics Engineering and M. Tech course in Industrial Drives R&D Control. The curriculum has been systematically designed to mould a competent Electrical engineer to cater the needs of industry and R&D.All Electrical maintenance and developmental activities within the campus is overseen and undertaken by the department.

Electronics and Communication Engineering

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering was established in the year 1991. The department offers 4 years Bachelors degree program and 2 years Masters degree program in two specializations, affiliated to Mahatma Gandhi University. The first M. Tech programme in Advanced Communication and Information Systems started in the year 2011 and second M. Tech programme in Advanced Electronics and Communication Engineering started in the year 2013.B. Tech in Electronics and Communication at RIT Kottayam is a sought after branch of study for the top raked students in the State of Kerala. Department of Electronics offers M. Tech in two specialisations and the annual intake is 18 students in both the specializations.

Mechanical Engineering

The Department of Mechanical Engineering has recorded a consistent growth over the years. The syllabus has been formed by giving more importance to management sciences, information technology and CAD/CAM, in addition to thermal sciences, production technology, optimization techniques, hydraulics, design engineering, and conventional engineering drawings. A full fledged computer centre with over 35 independent workstations and LAN, supporting more than 20 nodes with latest softwares like NISA, ANSYS, AUTOCAD , STARDYNE etc., and facilities like laser printers, scanners, CD Writers, leased line etc. are available. The department has independent reference library having more than 500 volumes.
